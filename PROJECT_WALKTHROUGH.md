# RiskLedger Project Walkthrough

## 60-Second Interview Script
RiskLedger is a fintech finance dashboard I built to show I can connect business logic to decision-making. It combines four workflows: executive KPI reporting, FP&A budget-vs-actual analysis, credit-risk monitoring, and AP/close operations. On the executive page, I summarize metrics like default rate, net loss rate, budget variance, and cash runway. In FP&A, I compare budget to actuals and flag departments as over budget if they exceed plan by more than 10%. In credit risk, I segment borrowers by score and DTI to show where defaults and charge-offs concentrate. In accounting operations, I track AP aging, reconciliation status, and close completion percentage. The goal is to show I can think like a junior analyst in risk, finance, and accounting—not just build UI.

## 2-Minute Interview Script
RiskLedger is a portfolio project designed around a realistic fintech business problem: how to grow a lending portfolio while controlling credit losses, operating spend, and month-end close risk.

I structured it into four modules.

First, the Executive Summary gives leadership-level visibility into the portfolio and finance health. It highlights total approved loan principal, default and net loss rates, budget variance, and runway so stakeholders can quickly identify whether risk and spending are trending in the right direction.

Second, the FP&A dashboard compares budgeted and actual performance. It calculates gross margin, net income, and variance percent, and uses practical flagging logic: over budget above +10%, on track within ±10%, under budget below -10%. That mirrors how analyst teams triage exceptions each month.

Third, the Credit Risk dashboard focuses on underwriting quality and expected returns. It tracks approval rate, default rate, and charge-off rate, then segments defaults by credit-score bands. I also calculate a simple borrower risk score to support analyst-style recommendations: low-risk profiles can be approved, medium-risk profiles go to manual review, and high-risk profiles get declined or tighter terms.

Fourth, the AP/Close tracker monitors accounting execution with AP aging buckets, overdue items, missing documentation, reconciliation rates, and close checklist completion. This mirrors operational finance controls that impact audit readiness and reporting timelines.

Overall, this project demonstrates skills relevant to fintech risk, FP&A, accounting operations, and finance operations internships: KPI design, business logic, structured analysis, and clear dashboard communication.
